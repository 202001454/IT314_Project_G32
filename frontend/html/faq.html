FAQ
What is a central mess management system?
A central mess management system is a software system that manages and streamlines the operations of a mess or cafeteria. It helps in managing the customers, view menu, payments, and other related tasks.
Who can use the central mess management system?
The central mess management system can be used by customers, cadets, and managers.
How to start the mess service as a customer?
As a customer you can signup in our website and verify your account. After completion of the login details, you have to make a payment to the manager and after successfully updation of your payment by manager, you can enjoy the mess service.
How do I provide feedback using the central mess management system?
you can provide feedback about their order using the central mess management system by rating the food, delivery, and overall experience.
Can I view the menu without creating an account?
Yes, you can view the everyday menu at the home page without login.
How to join the service as a new user?
As a new user you can signup in our website and verify your account. After completion of the signup, you have to make a payment to the manager and after successfully updation of your payment by manager, you can enjoy the mess service.
Do mess provide non-veg food?
No, We do not provide non-veg food.
Is the central mess management system secure?
Yes, the central mess management system is secure and ensures the safety and privacy of customer data and transactions.
