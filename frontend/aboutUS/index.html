Our Story

We started from the humble beginnings of a Mumbai-based tiffin service started by one man himself in his 1.5 BHK home, Saurabh Khare.

A little seed planted by Saurabh Khare's mother grew in his mind at an exponential rate. From tiffin deliveries to something of a tiffin restaurant, this little venture grew into what it is today. A PHD himself, he couldn't let this venture stay offline any longer. Thus, came forth this website and the ease of getting this service just by a few clicks.
